About Me:

I am a first-gen Mexican-American studying Geography at UCLA. I want to learn how to ice skate and pick up archery as a hobby someday! As a critical digital map maker my goal is to lend my skills to highlight my communities experiences. I believe its important to gather community input on issues that will directly impact them, by representing my communities voice through maps I hope to reasure them of their power. My skills as a map maker aside, I am also bilingual which allows me to bridge the language-communication gap I often see them encounter in their lives.
